# Hello YouTube Insert Extension

Extension nay bat video YouTube full man hinh (kieu meme) khi ban go tu `never` trong o nhap/chat tren website.
Neu go tu `main`, extension se dong tab hien tai (tuong duong `Ctrl+W`).

## Cai dat nhanh (Chrome/Edge)

1. Mo trang `chrome://extensions` (hoac `edge://extensions`).
2. Bat `Developer mode`.
3. Bam `Load unpacked`.
4. Chon thu muc:

```text
C:\Users\dmin\Downloads\IT\project\extension\hello-wave-extension
```

## Cach thu

1. Mo bat ky website co o nhap van ban (Facebook, Zalo Web, Gmail, Notion, ...).
2. Go bat ky noi dung co chua tu `never` (vi du: `abc never xyz`).
3. Extension se phu video YouTube len toan bo man hinh va tu dong phat.
4. Video dang duoc cau hinh: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hPr-Yc92qaY`
5. Neu noi dung co chua `main`, tab hien tai se dong ngay.

## Ghi chu

- Extension chi chay phia trinh duyet, khong gui du lieu ra ngoai.
- Kich hoat khi trong o nhap co xuat hien tu `never` o bat ky vi tri (khong phan biet hoa/thuong).
- Khong hien thanh dieu khien YouTube (tam dung/tua/seek).
- Da them crop + mask de giam toi da logo/share/text noi o mep video.
- Da toi uu referrer/origin de giam loi cau hinh trinh phat tren Facebook.
- Video duoc autoplay o che do mute de on dinh.
- Nhan phim `M` khi video dang hien de unmute + dat volume 100.
- Video giu full man hinh cho den khi nhan `Esc` de tat.
- Trigger `main` uu tien dong tab truoc trigger video.
- Trigger `main` duoc bat ca theo noi dung o nhap va theo chuoi phim go truc tiep.
